About Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, born on 17th September 1950, is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India. Known for his strong leadership and powerful oratory skills, he has brought major reforms and development projects across India.

He began his journey from a humble background, rising through hard work, discipline, and dedication to serve the nation. His personality inspires millions around the globe.

Admirable Qualities

  • Visionary Leadership: He introduced transformational schemes like Digital India, Make in India, and Swachh Bharat.
  • Hardworking: Works tirelessly and maintains a strict routine with discipline.
  • Patriotism: Always puts the country first in his decisions and actions.
  • Excellent Communicator: Connects with people through Mann Ki Baat and social media.
  • Inspirational Journey: From selling tea as a child to becoming PM.
